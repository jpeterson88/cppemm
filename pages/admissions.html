Admissions

Applicants are intestidually evaluated for admission into the Educational Multimedia graduate program considering the following criteria:

  • A GPA of 3.0 in the undergraduate work.
  • Academic and/or professional experience in the related fields is desirable.

In addition to submitting a university application, copies of academic transcripts, and application fee to the Admissions Office, applicants are encouraged to submit a personal statement, resume, and three reference letters in support of their applications to the Educational Multimedia Program Assistant, Kelly Mitchell (klmitchell@csupomona.edu or call 909-869-2358).
