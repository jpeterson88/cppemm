About The Program

Welcome to the Educational Multimedia program website at Cal Poly Pomona University. We are pleased that you are taking a few minutes of your time to explore our graduate program and see what we have to offer you.

The Educational Multimedia program is proud to offer coursework leading to a Master’s degree in Education. The focus of this production-based program is on the creative integration of graphics, animation, video, audio, text, and interactivity for educational and training settings based on the sound principles of instructional design. This is the only MA degree in the College of Education & Integrative Studies that does not require a teaching credential. Students develop theoretical foundations in creating Multimedia materials, enhance their technical skills and become involved in creating a variety of innovative interactive multimedia-based learning environments.

Our program focuses on innovative delivery of content to schools and corporate sector.

If you are interested in computer technology and production for educational and training settings or if you are an existing K-12 teacher, corporate trainer or consultant, the MA in Educational Multimedia will keep you on the “cutting edge” of the 21st century classroom or training room, preparing you for exciting career advancements.

This program is designed as a two-year graduate degree program for working professionals with flexibility for those who are interested in completing their degree quicker or over a longer period of time. All coursework is offered in the evenings.
